About the series

Independent analysis.
No agenda.

SME Gems surfaces lesser-known companies on India's BSE and NSE SME platforms that demonstrate mainboard-quality fundamentals — strong and expanding margins, growing order books, promoters with demonstrated execution capabilities, and structural competitive advantages.

Each insight covers company journey, business model, financial performance, growth roadmap, peer comparison, substantiated and speculation averse risk analysis, and an investor execution checklist. All content is created independently. No paid promotions.

Metrics shown on cards may reflect more recent figures than the ones documented in the reports.
