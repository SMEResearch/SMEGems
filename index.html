About the series

Independent research.
No agenda.

SME Gems surfaces under-researched companies on India's BSE and NSE SME platforms that demonstrate mainboard-quality fundamentals — strong and expanding margins, growing order books, promoters with demonstrated execution capabilities, and structural competitive advantages.

Each report covers company journey, business model, financial performance, growth roadmap, peer comparison, substantiated and speculation averse risk analysis, and an investor execution checklist. All research is independent. There is no paid promotion.

Metrics shown on cards may reflect more recent figures than the ones documented in the reports.
